Places I've Lived

Sullivan, Missouri

Sullivan is a rural town in Missouri with a population of just under 6,000. I lived here up until 18, when I moved away to college. I enjoyed living in a small peaceful town but also vowed never to move back there.

Things to do in Sullivan:
  • Drive a truck
  • Shoot things

      • St. Louis, Missouri

      St. Louis has been my home a couple different times. I lived here while attending college and moved back here a couple years ago.

      Things to do in St. Louis:
      • Be a Cardinals fan
      • Hate the Cubs

Springfield, Illinois

Springfield is the capital of Illinois and a decent size city surrounded by rural farming communities. I lived here twice and both times were disappointing.

Things to do in Springfield:
  • Visit Lincoln monuments
  • Watch corn grow

      • Eau Claire, Wisconsin

      Eau Claire is a small city, about 70,000 people, a couple hours east of Minneapolis. It's somewhat a college town and has beautiful scenery. I lived here during their worst winter in 30 years so I didn't have a lot of time to enjoy said scenery.

      Things to do in Eau Claire:
      • Shovel snow
      • Pray for no snow
